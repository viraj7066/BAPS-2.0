About BAPS

The BAPS Swaminarayan Sanstha provides for the spiritual, cultural, physical, and emotional needs of people from all walks of life.

PSM 100 Gallery

  • Children's Adventureland

    The Children’s Cultural Land or ‘Bal Nagari’, spread over 17 acres and consisting of 3 domes, are managed by children

  • Swaminarayan Akshardham, New Delhi – Replica

    67-feet high massive replica of Swaminarayan Akshardham, New Delhi.

  • Exhibition Pavilions

    Six immersive shows and live performances themed on relevant topics taught and promoted by Pramukh Swami Maharaj

  • Cultural Gates

    A total of seven large decorative gateways welcome and guide visitors to the various zones of the festival campus.

  • Glow Garden

    This glow garden has been divided into six themes: Bhagwan, Guru, Scriptures, Nation, World and Nature.

  • Light and Sound Show

    Experience a spectacular light and sound show that pays tribute to this quality of Pramukh Swami Maharaj.

  • Pramukh Swami Maharaj Maha-Murti

    The 30 feet high murti of Pramukh Swami Maharaj is perched on a 15 feet high and 40 feet wide pedestal.

  • Shantinu Dham – Chansad

    Born in the village of Chansad, Pramukh Swami Maharaj’s childhood name was Shantilal.

  • Swaminarayan Vishwashanti Mahayag

    Born in the village of Chansad, Pramukh Swami Maharaj’s childhood name was Shantilal.

  • Pramukhraj Dhol Pathak

    A Khandeshi Dhol Tasha Pathak who wins the heart of audiences at psm 100 nagar with its unbeliveble beats of dhol and tasha.

  • The Village of Buzo

    An eye-opening live presentation with videos deliver the message of parents’ sacrifice for their children.

  • The Jungle of Sheru

    This is a performance showcasing the importance of a guru in one’s life through the tale of a young lion.
